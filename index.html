❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

After placing your order, you will receive our payment email address and your unique order ID. Log into your online banking, send an e-Transfer for the exact order total, and include your order ID in the message field. We auto-deposit — no security question needed.
Orders where payment is received before 2 PM EST ship the same business day. Payments after 2 PM ship the next business day. You will receive a tracking number by email once your order is dispatched. Delivery is typically 2–5 business days within Canada.
All compounds are ≥98% purity, with most achieving ≥99%. Each batch is tested by third-party HPLC and mass spectrometry. A Certificate of Analysis (CoA) is available on request and included in your shipment.
Lyophilized (dry) peptides should be stored in a freezer at –20°C for long-term storage. Once reconstituted with bacteriostatic water, store at 4°C in the refrigerator and use within 30 days. Keep away from light and moisture.
Currently we ship within Canada only. We are working on expanding international shipping. Join our newsletter to be notified when international orders become available.
In the rare event your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, contact us within 72 hours of the expected delivery date. We will investigate and, where appropriate, reship at no additional cost to you.
